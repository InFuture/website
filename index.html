What is InFuture?

InFuture is a hackathon that will gather 300 of the nation's most passionate and innovative high school students for an amazing event on February 27th - 28th, 2016.

Frequenty Asked Questions

Why are we doing this?

We want high school students to have a way to express their ideas in a professional environment, as college students and adults often have. We believe they all can innovate the future.

Where and when?

InFuture will take place on February 27th - 28th, 2016 in WeWork, New York City.

Who is behind this?

InFuture is organized by Jay Mo, Liam Rahav, Branodn Yu, Grace Zhang, Amy Sorto, Michael Zhang, and Hack Gen Y.

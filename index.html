What is InFuture?

InFuture is a hackathon that will gather 300 of the nation's most passionate and innovative high school students for an amazing event on February 27th - 28th, 2016.

Change your life in one weekend

Frequenty Asked Questions

Why are we doing this?

We want high school students to have a way to express their ideas in a professional environment, as college students and adults often have. We believe they all can innovate the future.

Where and when?

InFuture will take place on February 27th - 28th, 2016 in WeWork, New York City.

Who is behind this?

InFuture is organized by Jay Mo, Liam Rahav, Branodn Yu, Grace Zhang, Amy Sorto, Michael Zhang, and Hack Gen Y.

Who can apply?

Every high school students are welcome to apply! Anyone beyond is welcome to apply as a mentor. If you're unsure if you are eligible, shoot us an email.

What should I bring? How much does the event cost?

You should bring your laptop and charger, school ID, positive attitude, and more - see the recommend list here. This event is completely free, and food will be provided!

Do I need a team?

Single person teams are allowed, but we encourage you to work in group! Teams can have up to four people spanning any schools.

Will there be food?

All meals, snacks, and drinks will be provided for the entire weekend. If you have a dietary restriction, please make sure to mention it in the application. There will be a wide variety of food.

Can I temporarily leave the event?

Unfortunately due to liability issues, you may not temporarily leave the event unless given consent by an organizer.

What is InFuture?

InFuture is a hackathon that will gather 300 of the nation's most passionate and innovative high school students for an amazing event on February 27th - 28th, 2016.

Frequenty Asked Questions

Why are we doing this?

We believe that high schoolers should be able to express their ideas in the same manner as college students and adults. The innovators of the future should be able to make an impact now.

Where and when?

InFuture will take place on February 27th - 28th, 2016 in WeWork, New York City.

Who is behind this?

InFuture is organized by a team of passionate high school students in collaboration with Hacking Generation Y.

Who can apply?

Every high school student is welcome to apply! Anyone beyond is welcome to apply as a mentor. If you're unsure if you are eligible, shoot us an email.

What should I bring? How much does the event cost?

You should bring your laptop and charger, school ID, positive attitude, and more - see the list of recommended things here. This event is completely free, and food will be provided!

Do I need a team?

You do not need to have a team before the event! Teams of up to four people are allowed, and we highly recommend you join one. We'll have networking sessions during the hackathon to help you find others looking for a team.

Will there be food?

All meals, snacks, and drinks will be provided for the entire weekend. If you have a dietary restriction, please make sure to mention it in the application. There will be a wide variety of food.

Can I temporarily leave the event?

Unfortunately, due to liability issues, you may not temporarily leave the event unless given consent by an organizer. If you have something you need to get, notify an organizer and they will provide it for you.

Are there hardware or prizes?

The exact hardware kits and prizes will be announced at a later date, but the answer is definitely yes.

Will there be travel reimbursement?

More information will be released soon.
